Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

ArthaldInvitation uses Pinterest and Etsy integrations to help manage and publish content for our own digital invitation products.

Information We Access

When authorized by the account owner, the app may access Pinterest and Etsy account information, listing details, listing images, boards, and Pins needed to create and publish content.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to create, schedule, organize, and publish Pins and to manage Etsy listing-related content for ArthaldInvitation.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We do not share accessed information with third parties except as required to operate the Pinterest and Etsy integrations.

Data Retention

We retain only the minimum information necessary to operate the app and its scheduling features. Tokens and related connection data may be stored securely for ongoing authorized access.

User Control

You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest or Etsy account settings or by disconnecting the app.
